Paghetti Bolognaise

Ingredientes 1 Onion 1 Carrot
2 rashers bacon 1 x 400g tin tomatoes
500g mince 1 tbsp oil

Method

1. Peel and chop onion and carrot, chop bacon
2. Heat oil in a pan. fry onion. carrot and bacon until bacon changes colour
3. Add tomatoes and mince
4. Simmer gently until the carrot is soft and the mince is cooked
5. Stir the mixture occasionally
6. Serve with cooked pasta
